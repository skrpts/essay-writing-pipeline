## Capability

Takes a thesis statement and supporting arguments and arranges them into a logical essay structure with clear transitions and balanced coverage.

## When to Use

- Planning essay structure after thesis development
- Reorganising a draft that lacks coherence
- Checking argument flow before writing

## Inputs

Thesis statement, list of arguments and evidence, word count target, essay type (argumentative, analytical, compare/contrast)

## Outputs

Detailed essay outline with introduction, body paragraphs (each with topic sentence, evidence, and analysis), and conclusion
