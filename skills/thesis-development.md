## Capability

Takes broad or vague essay topics and refines them into specific, arguable thesis statements with clear scope and direction.

## When to Use

- Starting a new essay or research paper
- Refining a topic after initial research
- Strengthening a weak or unfocused thesis

## Inputs

Broad topic or question, subject area, assignment requirements, preliminary reading notes

## Outputs

Focused thesis statement, scope definition, key arguments to explore, and suggested counter-arguments to address
